About ALON

02

ALON (Always On) is a personal AI assistant for the entire home-buying journey. Built by TryThat.ai, ALON guides buyers through all 8 stages of property acquisition — from initial search to final possession — acting exclusively in the buyer's interest.

Unlike traditional real estate platforms that serve listings, ALON serves buyers. It never shares your contact without permission, provides complete journey support, and always works for you — never the builder or agent.

The design language reflects Trust Architecture — a visual system built on deep navy foundations and warm terracotta accents that convey institutional reliability with approachable warmth.

Design Principles
Trust First
Every visual choice reinforces trust. Navy = institutional confidence, Terracotta = warmth.
Flat Over Skeuomorphic
Subtle elevation only. On blue bg: border + opacity, no shadows.
One CTA Rule
One primary action per screen. Secondary = outlined/ghost for hierarchy.
Active = Border + Fill
Selected states: both border color AND background fill change. Never just color.
